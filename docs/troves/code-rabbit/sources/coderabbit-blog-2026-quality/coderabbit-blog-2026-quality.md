# 2025 was the year of AI speed. 2026 will be the year of AI quality.

## Key Points
- **Shift in Focus**: 2025 was characterized by a focus on speed in AI-assisted development, but 2026 will prioritize quality as engineering organizations shift from "how fast can we generate code?" to "how confident can we be in the code we ship?"
- **Trust Gap**: The rapid adoption of AI coding tools has led to a trust gap, with CTOs and senior engineering leaders spending significant time cleaning up AI mistakes.
- **Market Signal**: The shift from speed to quality is driven by the need for correctness and maintainability, not just raw throughput.
- **CodeRabbit's Role**: CodeRabbit is positioned to help teams address the quality gap by providing reliable, context-aware code reviews.

## Evidence
- **AWS re:Invent**: CodeRabbit themed their booth around the "Vibe Code Cleanup Specialist" meme, sparking conversations with CTOs about the challenges of cleaning up AI-generated code.
- **Adoption Metrics**: Microsoft and Google reported that AI is generating 30% of their code, indicating widespread adoption and the need for quality control.

## Implications
- **KPIs**: Engineering leaders are shifting KPIs from raw throughput to indicators of correctness and maintainability.
- **Tooling**: Tools like CodeRabbit that focus on quality, context, and reliability will become increasingly important.
